Gem Merchant

You are a merchant of luxury, and a responsible one at that! You strive to sell a specific amount of precious gems to meet your needs; however, you recognize that absolute wealth is immoral, so you must not be greedy and sell above your sales goal.

Click on a gem to sell it - its value will then be added to your sales total.

Win the game by meeting your sales goal exactly. Lose the game by exceeding your sales goal (you covetous pig!)

The market is dynamic! Each round, your gems & sales goals will be appraised at a different value.
